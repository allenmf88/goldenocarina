Ocarina of Time

Story
As a young child of about 11, Link awakens to find a guardian fairy, Navi, had finally come to him. Link was instructed to rid a curse from his mentor, the Deku Tree. After doing so, the Deku Tree gave Link a Spiritual Stone of the Forest and tells him to show it to Hyrule’s princess, Zelda. Link finds Zelda and her nursemaid, Impa. They tell him of an evil man, Ganondorf Dragmire, who wants to break into the Sacred Realm and steal its ultimate relic, the Triforce. Link tries to beat Ganondorf to the Triforce, but after opening the Sacred Realm, Link’s spirit was put into suspended animation before he could get the Triforce and Ganondorf stole it. Link came to 7 years later and is told by the keeper of the Sacred Realm, Rauru, to awaken 5 sages to help him banish Ganondorf. Before they could succeed, Ganondorf uses the Triforce to transform into a hideous beast, Ganon. Link, Zelda and the Sages banished Ganon into the Evil Realm. After, Link went back 7 years to regain his lost time.
