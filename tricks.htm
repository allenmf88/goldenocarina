TRICKS

 

Swordless Link- When Ganon knocks the master sword out of your hands (Not Ganondorf) quickly save the game and reset the N64. When that same file is opened Link should have no sword. This trick will lead to many tricks and glitches.

Blue Mist- While playing Horseback Archery play the song of storms and a blue mist will appear( You must perform the swordless link trick before trying this trick and you have tell the lady you are going to play the Horseback Archery game before performing the trick.)

See what Link sees- To do this trick you must have the swordless Link trick. First while your on Epona equip your deku nuts and longshot. Then throw a deku nut, bring out your longshot, and then throw another deku nut. Link will notice he is being watched. You will then see what Link sees.

Kakariko Mountain- First go to Kakariko village get on top of the house with the man sitting on it and look up at the wind mill and you should see two black spaces. On the one on the bottom left there should be a wooden thing. Longshot yourself on that and you will be inside the top of the wind mill! Now equip your hover boots and climb the two stair cases and there should be a cucco. Pick it up and jump out  the other black space that was right in front of the cucco. Land on the archery building. Then fly onto the cucco barn. Now fly off it and land on that huge hill. There you are on top of Kakariko Mountain!

"Wow, that's a funny mask- Go to the market and put on the mask of truth and go to that girl who is chasing the cucco around. Talk to her twice and then take off the Mask of Truth. Talk to her one more time and then she will say "Wow, that's a funny mask!

Hidden Cave in Zora's Fountain- This trick allows you to get into a secret cave in Zora's Fountain. First of all, go to Zora's Fountain, and stand on the iceberg where Lord Jabu-Jabu used to live. Using the Up C Button, look to the right on the wall, and you should see an opening in the wall. Dive into the water, then swim over to the island that has a cave leading to Goron City. You should see a gray boulder. Use you Golden Gauntlets to lift the boulder, than throw it. An opening will appear. Fall down into the opening, then put on your Lens of Truth. defeat the Skulltulas, the head up the slope to defeat the last Skulltula. You are now in the opening you saw from the iceberg.

Killer Runner-  While you are a child, go to Hyrule Field during the day. If and when you see the running man, stand a distance in front of where he is going to run. Then, when he hits you can get anywhere from 5-3 hearts COMPLETELY taken away!

Get Epona Without Jumping The Fence- There is a way to get Epona in Lon Lon Ranch without jumping the fences. After racing Ingo and beating him twice, you will get Epona but will be locked in the ranch. Normally, you have to jump over one of the fences. If you find this difficult, you can save and restart your game. Leave the Temple of Time and go into Hyrule Field. Use your ocarina and play Epona's Song to call Epona. She should come to you. (Note: Try to be near the middle of Hyrule Field when using Epona's Song. If you are too close to the castle, Epona will not be able to reach you.

Swordless LInk 2- To do this, you must first do the Fishing Trick where you get your pole out of the pond (Below). Go across the narrow part of the lake to the right of the fishing place. Go to a spot between the fence where the scarecrows are and the wall. Pull out the fishing rod and cast. Link will sit there like he's fishing. Save your game. If this trick worked there will be a Deku stick where your sword should be. In the menu the Master Sword will be gone. Don't try and pull out a Deku stick because it will freeze and you will have to turn the power off and then back on (reset wont work). The way to get your sword back is to put it back in the Temple of Time and then pull it out again. Don't do this with Biggorons sword. If you do it will still be in the menu but you cant select it and Biggoron wont give you another one.

NOTE TO THE READER: There is obviously many areas in Zelda 64 where you can get unlimited rupees. I will list a few areas here, but not all. I will post a notice when I have recieved enough.

1- This trick will allow you to get unlimited money. Once you have traveled to Hyrule market, this trick can take affect. When you first enter the market, enter the door to the right, and their will be pots. They are full of money. Destroy them all for 20+ rupees. Once you've gained all the money, exit and go in again. The pots full of money will be back! do this and evantually you will have max rupees.

2- To do this trick you will need to be by the entrance to the Hyrule kingdom, just walk up the chains that hold the bridge untill you reach the wall then fall down to:

- If you're on the right chain to your left
- If you're on the left chain to your right

You will take damage unless you press both A and B buttons right before you land and that'll soften the fall. You should get one red ruppie (red ruppee = 20 rupees) for every fall. Enjoy

3- You must be adult link to do this trick. After completing the sleeping man and little girl sections of Adult Link's Trading Sequence, go once again to the screen 1 left of the starting point of the Lost Woods. Kill the skull kid there using your sword and he'll drop 200 rupees! Leave the woods and go back and kill him again for another 200! You'll have a full 500 in seconds!

4- When you are Adult Link or kid Link, find the guy kneeling down who looks like he's begging. For kid Link, he is The Market, adult link, he is in Kariko Village. Remember where he was. Then go into one of the numerous caves from the holes in the ground, and destroy a bugs nest (big brown beehive looking things in top corners of most caves). Bottle a bug. If you see a fish in one of the puddles, bottle it instead. if you have more than one bottle, exit the cave and come back for another fish. Or you can release a bug, and re-catch the ones that come out. bring the bottled goodies to the begging guy. He will now ask you to sel him the contents of your bottle(s). Select the fish or bug, and use it on him. He will give you 50 rupees for a bug and 100 rupees for a fish. this is really good for when you are low on cash and need to buy items.

5- When you are a kid, go to Hyrule Castle during the day. Walk up onto one of the chains that holds up the drawbridge. Now play the 'Sun Song', making it night. When the drawbridge raises, you will go up with it. When the draw bridge reaches the top, walk along the edge of the drawbridge. You will get 60 rupies for doing this. When you are done, drop off the edge into the castle (Press A + B when landing so you don't get hurt) and do it again. This is a fast, easy way to get rupies.
