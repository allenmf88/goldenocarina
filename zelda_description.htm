ZELDA DESCRIPTION

The Legend of Zelda: Ocarina of Time

Publisher: Nintendo
Release Date: November 1998 (N64)
Genre: Adventure

The wait is almost over for millions of anxious N64 owners. The release of The Legend of Zelda: Ocarina of Time is right around the corner and it appears as though the wait was worth it. Using 256 megabits of memory, Zelda promises to be the largest N64 title yet. Players will be immersed in spectacular 3-D landscapes ranging from dark forests to quaint towns. There are plenty of new features including time travel which will allow you to play as both a young and adult Link.
